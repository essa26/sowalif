About AlDewan

A final project for the bootcamp. Something that was supposedly simple to make in a relatively short period of time. The bootcamp gave us the know-how to make a wide variety of websites, we had to come up with the ideas.

The idea started off with an inspiration from Mshary's project (agulex.com) and took off from there in combination with Mohammad's previous work with image posting. Essa was a champ. It was to be a hybrid between a messaging board and a social network, with a user shaping his own experience.

With input from all members on the project's evolution, the website was formed. Users have the ability of posting anonymously but signing up enables greater levels of interaction. In the end, it is all up to the user.
